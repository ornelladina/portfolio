Case Study: Profix Handyman Services CA

RELIABLE REPAIRS, ELEVATED.

Project Overview

Profix Handyman Services provides professional home maintenance and repair solutions in Orange County. The objective was to create a digital storefront that builds instant trust and streamlines the lead generation process.

Design Strategy

Focused on "conversion-first" design. By utilizing high-contrast call-to-actions (the signature teal) and clear, service-oriented imagery, the site bridges the gap between a casual browser and a booked client.
