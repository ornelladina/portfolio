Case Study: Aethel High-Fashion

AETHEL: THE ART OF LUXURY SHOPPING

Project Overview: AETHEL

Role: UI/UX Designer.

Objective: To design a high-end fashion e-commerce application that prioritizes brand curation and a premium, editorial shopping experience.

Design Philosophy: Focused on a "soft luxury" aesthetic, utilizing a minimalist layout, muted color palettes, and high-contrast typography to emphasize exclusivity and class.

1. Challenge & Goals

Challenge: High-fashion e-commerce platforms often prioritize quantity over quality, leading to overwhelming interfaces that detract from the exclusivity of the brand.

Goals: To create a 'soft luxury' mobile experience where the visual storytelling remains the primary focus. The goal was to build a cohesive visual system that bridges the gap between editorial-style browsing and efficient, high-end mobile commerce for luxury shopping specifically.

2. Market Analysis


I analyzed existing luxury fashion apps to identify common pitfalls in mobile layout design. My focus was on how top-tier brands balance dense product grids with white space, and how they handle CTA placement to maintain a premium feel without sacrificing utility.

Key Findings:

A. The "Soft Luxury" Gap: There is an opportunity for a platform that balances a refined, minimalist aesthetic with intuitive mobile-first patterns, specifically by using whitespace and controlled typography to elevate the perceived value of items.

VISUAL DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Visual HierarchyA clear, tiered layout guides the user's eye from the brand identity to product details, ensuring the most essential information is prioritized for seamless navigation.

Strategic Use of WhitespaceGenerous negative space surrounds each high-fashion asset, allowing products to breathe and reinforcing the premium, exclusive nature of the brand.

Consistency & AlignmentA strict grid system maintains perfect order across all screens, creating a reliable, high-end shopping experience that feels intentional and polished.

Color Contrast & MoodThe dark-toned palette evokes sophistication and luxury, while carefully balanced light-colored typography ensures that primary calls to action remain accessible and legible.

Proximity & GroupingRelated elements, such as pricing and action buttons, are grouped together to reduce cognitive load, helping users intuitively understand the interface at a glance.
