AETHEL: THE ART OF CURATED LUXURY

Project Overview

  • Role: UI/UX Designer
  • Objective: A high-end fashion e-commerce experience.
  • Design Philosophy: Soft luxury, minimalist, editorial.

1. Challenge & Goals

To bridge the gap between editorial-style browsing and efficient, high-end mobile commerce while maintaining brand exclusivity.

Visual Design Principles

Visual Hierarchy: A tiered layout guiding the eye from identity to details.

Strategic Whitespace: Generous space to allow products to breathe.

Color Mood: Deep Burgundy backgrounds with Champagne typography for sophistication.

Color Palette

Deep Burgundy: #2A1B1B

Off-White: #EAE3D5

Champagne: #C5A880
