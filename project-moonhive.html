MOONHIVE

Brand Mission & Concept

Moonhive is a wellness brand, bridging ancient apothecary traditions with mindful recovery. Moonhive offers immersive self-care experiences centered on nature and the lunar cycle.

Brand Typography

1. Logo Font: Magensburg

2. Title Font: Quattrocento

3. Text Font: Instrument Sans

Color Palette

Brand DNA

Packaging Design

The packaging focuses on material contrast to align with the brand's visual identity. By combining raw, textured paper with a metallic gold wax seal, the design creates a tactile link between the brand logo and the product, reinforcing the established aesthetic.
